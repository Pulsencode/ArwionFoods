Kerala Sadya

A traditional feast served on a banana leaf, featuring a variety of vegetarian dishes.

Kerala Sadya includes rice served with numerous side dishes such as sambar, avial, and payasam, showcasing the rich flavors of Kerala cuisine.

Fish Curry

A spicy and tangy curry made with fresh catch from the backwaters.

Kerala Fish Curry is prepared using coconut milk, tamarind, and a blend of local spices, providing a unique taste that reflects the coastal flavors.

Appam with Stew

Soft, fluffy hoppers served with a savory stew of vegetables or meat.

Appam is a rice flour pancake that pairs perfectly with the rich and aromatic stew, making it a popular breakfast dish in Kerala.

Puttu and Kadala

Steamed rice cake served with black chickpeas curry.

Puttu is a healthy breakfast option, layered with coconut and served with kadala curry, providing a perfect blend of flavors.

Dosa and Sambar

Crispy rice crepes served with a flavorful lentil soup.

Dosa, a popular South Indian dish, is served with sambar and coconut chutney, offering a delightful taste that is both crispy and spicy.
